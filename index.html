Kenz Coffee House

Premium Yemeni Coffee - 100% Organic

The Treasure of Yemen

"Kenz" translates to Treasure in Arabic. Founded by Abdulnaser Alnajjar and Moshtak Alamri, our mission is to bring the rich, ancient traditions of Yemeni coffee culture to the heart of Canton, Michigan. We source 100% organic beans directly from Yemeni farmers to ensure a world-class experience in every cup.
